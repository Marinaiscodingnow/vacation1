- My Summer Vacation -

I spent the majority of my summer at my grandparents house in upstate New York, touring colleges, and staying in New Jersey. Yet I was able to go on a trip to Lake Placid and then to Montreal, Canada. Having lots of fun and taking many photos along the way.



- Upstate New York -

The biggest part of my summer, I spent around 1 month there. My grandparents own chickens, which are super fun to feed and we were able to collect their eggs. My grandparents have an amazing view and mid-summer there were beautiful sunsets almost every night. Besides going to the lake and enjoying the property, we were also able to go to farm day. An aunnual event where families get to go to different farms and see the animals, crops, and feilds.

- Lake Placid & Canada -

My family went to Lake Placid for my Dad who participated in an Iron man. A competition where contestants had to swim 2 miles, bike 106 miles, and then run a full marathon. It was super fun to watch the race and support our dad. After the race, we drove up to Montreal where we saw the sights of the city. We went to a mueseum of art, walked around a nearby park, and got to see the overall sites of the city.

- New Jersey -

I didn't spent much time in New Jersey, only a few weeks at the beggining of summer vacation. However, I still was able to have a lot of fun. I got to see my best friend who came up from florida, go to the beach, and go bike riding. Over all it was a really fun vacation.
